## 📅 Meeting Schedule

### Daily
- **Standup**: 09:00 AM UTC (15 min)
- All team members share progress and blockers

### Weekly
- **Monday Planning**: 10:00 AM UTC (1 hour)
- Team leads plan the week
- **Friday Review**: 4:00 PM UTC (30 min)
- Week wrap-up with full team

### Monthly
- **Retrospective**: Last Friday, 2:00 PM UTC (2 hours)
- Review month, celebrate wins, plan next month

### Quarterly
- **Planning Summit**: 1st week (full day)
- Strategic planning for next quarter

## 🔑 Key Principles

### Work With Us

✅ **We believe in:**
- **Collaboration** - Working together, not in silos
- **Transparency** - Open communication, honest feedback
- **Quality** - Excellence in all work
- **Growth** - Supporting each other's development
- **Accountability** - Taking responsibility for results
- **Respect** - Valuing all perspectives

### How We Communicate

**Daily**: Standup meetings, Slack updates  
**Weekly**: Planning and review meetings  
**Monthly**: Full retrospectives and planning  
**Always**: Direct, honest, respectful

### How We Make Decisions

1. **Routine** (role owner decides) - Same day
2. **Important** (stakeholder input) - 24-48 hours
3. **Critical** (team consensus) - 3-5 days

See [Team Coordination](TEAM_COORDINATION.md) for details.
