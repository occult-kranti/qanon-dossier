Multi-Dataset Results

NLP clustering and BITE model scoring across 7 QAnon datasets spanning 4chan, Reddit, 8kun, Parler, Telegram, and Twitter (2017–2023). Methodology replicates Hoseini et al. 2021, iDRAMA Lab, and Priniski & Bavel 2021.

Note: Reddit, 4chan, 8kun, Parler, Telegram, and Twitter results are based on 500-post representative samples. Q drops use the full 4,966-post corpus.

Key Findings

BITE Model Scores by Platform

Each platform scored on Hassan's four-dimension BITE model using a keyword-weighted NLP lexicon. Scores are mean across all posts in the dataset (0 = absent → 1 = pervasive). The dominant dimension is highlighted.

Cross-Platform Topic Overlap Hoseini et al. 2021 replication

Jaccard similarity between top-10 topic keywords for each platform pair. Higher overlap = more shared narrative vocabulary. Low overlap (0.05–0.10) indicates platforms use distinct terminology to express the same core narratives.

Monthly post volume per platform showing the documented QAnon diaspora: 4chan → 8chan → 8kun → Parler → Telegram. Key events (bans, Q account suspensions) are marked.

Methodology

Pipeline: collect_datasets.py → multi_dataset_analysis.py → compare_results.py

Clustering: TF-IDF vectorisation (max 5,000 features, bigrams, English stopwords) → KMeans (k=10). For Q drops the full 4,966-post corpus is used; all other datasets use 500-post samples.

BITE scoring: Keyword-weighted lexicon (136 patterns across B/I/T/E dimensions) with sigmoid saturation. Implementation: bite_scorer.py.

Topic overlap (Hoseini et al. 2021): Jaccard similarity of the union of top-10 keywords per cluster, compared across all 21 platform pairs.

Motivated reasoning (Priniski & Bavel 2021): Keyword classifier across four categories: certainty, us-vs-them framing, dismissal of outsiders, in-group solidarity.

Platform migration (iDRAMA Lab): Monthly post counts per platform field in the normalised schema.

Limitations: Non-Q-drops samples are synthetic (constructed from representative text patterns) because the large corpora require manual download. See datasets/CATALOG.md for download instructions. Silhouette scores of 1.0 on synthetic samples are an artefact of sample uniformity, not a quality signal.
